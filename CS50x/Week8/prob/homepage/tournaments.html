Billiards Tournaments

Notable Billiards Tournaments

  • World Snooker Championship: An annual event organized by WPBSA.
  • Mosconi Cup: Annual nine-ball pool competition between Europe and the U.S.
  • WPA World Nine-ball Championship: Major international nine-ball pool tournament.
  • UMB World Championships: Various championships in carom billiards disciplines.

For more information, visit official websites of relevant billiards organizations.
